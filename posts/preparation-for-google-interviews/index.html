A study plan for google interview in 2 weeks.

2023-04-05 ::

First off, I am glad that my resume was selected for a google interview.

I have never really prepared for interviews. It has been a long time and I am doing this to keep my chances of being employable and being employed.

First, I am starting off with the cracking the coding interview book.
