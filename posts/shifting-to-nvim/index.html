My journey of switching code editors

2023-04-02 ::

So sometime back , I decided to switch to the latest hotshot meme that I witnessed.

I was casually browsing Twitch, bored out of my mind looking for something fun.

I for a random reason decided to search for “Software” and I saw the primeagen streaming..

A goofball coder with Dr Disrespect asthetics. But also works at Netflix and has a solid knowledge of what he is doing with the code.

And he was using terminal to edit the code.

This was not totally new to me.

I have heard of vi and nano before and have used them in my servers where the GUI were not available.

It’s been some time since I have used nano. Haven’t used it in the last 6 years to be exact, when I made the move to switch from Ubuntu to Arch Linux.

I had not always known how to exit vi but I figured it out soon.
